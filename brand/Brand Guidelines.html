§ 02 — Design Philosophy
The product gives people their hours back. The brand should do the same with their attention. No hyperactive gradients, no glowing orbs, no synthetic blue, no "AI" iconography. Nothing that signals automation by mimicking a robot.

Instead: paper, ink, daylight. Type with the manners of a printed book. Calm is the differentiator. Reliability is shown by restraint, not by saying "reliable" ten times.
— 01
Quiet, not silent.
Whitespace is the loudest thing on the page. Every element earns its presence.
— 02
Made, not generated.
Choices that look hand-set. No filler shapes, no decorative AI iconography.
— 03
Slow rhythm.
Generous leading, long line-lengths broken with care. Reading should feel like exhaling.
— 04
One ink, one accent.
Never more than two colours carrying meaning at once. Restraint reads as confidence.
